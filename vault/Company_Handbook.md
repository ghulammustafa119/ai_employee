# Company Handbook — Rules of Engagement

## Communication Rules
- Always be polite and professional in all messages
- Reply to emails within 24 hours
- Flag urgent messages immediately

## Approval Thresholds
| Action | Auto-Approve | Requires Approval |
|--------|-------------|-------------------|
| Email replies | To known contacts | New contacts, bulk sends |
| Payments | < $50 recurring | All new payees, > $100 |
| Social media | — | All posts, replies, DMs |
| File operations | Create, read | Delete, move outside vault |

## LinkedIn Posting Rules
- Post about business topics only
- Keep tone professional and engaging
- Maximum 1 post per day
- All posts require human approval before publishing

## Sensitive Keywords (trigger approval)
- payment, invoice, transfer, send money
- delete, remove, cancel
- reply, respond, message (to new contacts)
- post, publish, share (on social media)

## Working Hours
- Active monitoring: 8:00 AM - 10:00 PM
- Overnight: Queue items, process in morning
